Brand Story

The meaning behind our distinctive identity

WaterMark combines strength and connection, represented by our distinctive ampersand logo. The "&" symbolizes partnerships, collaboration, and the seamless integration of services we provide.

The square frame represents stability, reliability, and the structured approach we bring to every project. Together, these elements create a balanced and meaningful visual identity that resonates with our values and mission.

Our brand essence is built on connecting ideas, people, and solutions in an environment of trust and professional excellence. Every element of our visual identity reinforces this commitment.

Logo Usage Guidelines

Proper application ensures brand consistency

Clear Space

Always maintain minimum clear space equal to 1/4 of the logo height around all sides of the logo.

Minimum Size

Never use the logo smaller than 24px (digital) or 0.25 inches (print) in height.

Usage Don'ts

Never stretch, distort, recolor, or add effects to the logo. Do not recreate or modify the logo elements.
